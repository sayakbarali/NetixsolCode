HTML Bidirectional Text Tags

Bidirectional Override (BDO)

By default, this text follows the document's direction: This text is forced to display right-to-left.

Example of overriding text direction: הטקסט הזה מציג משמאל לימין

Bidirectional Isolation (BDI)

In this example, the text direction of the user's name does not interfere with the rest of the sentence:
User name: علي commented on your post.

Another example: 123-456 is the user's ID, ensuring proper alignment regardless of direction.
